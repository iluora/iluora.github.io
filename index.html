Fixify

Fixify is a powerful Unity Editor extension designed to analyze, diagnose, and optimize your Unity project. It helps developers maintain project health by identifying and fixing common issues such as missing references, unused assets, optimization problems, version control inconsistencies, and more. With a clean and intuitive interface, Fixify provides detailed diagnostics and repair tools, making it an essential asset for Unity projects of all sizes.
